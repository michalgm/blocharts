Greek <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rebetiko">Rebetiko</a> folk song dating back to at least 1927, claimed by many composers and cultures, with many Arabic, Jewish (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Klezmer">Klezmer</a>) and Turkish versions, but best known today through <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dick_Dale">Dick Dale</a>'s 1962 American surf rock interpretation. (From <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Misirlou">wikipedia entry</a>.)
