Traditional African-American spiritual, used in baptisms and declaration of the &#8220;born-again&#8221; process in Christianity.  From the 1930&#8217;s on, jazz musicians have often changed the rhythm to fit the &#8220;swing&#8221; meter of jazz composition.  Soloists and arrangers often quote another spiritual, &#8220;Joshua Fought the Battle of Jericho&#8221; to add variety to the piece.  BLO also quotes the riff from the Bob Marley song, 'Exodus.'
