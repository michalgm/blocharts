Famous anthem of the Civil Rights Movements of the 1960&#8217;s and 1970&#8217;s.  Sung by many vocal groups of the Black freedom movement, jazz musicians such as Abbey Lincoln and Nina Simone sang the song in a jazz context.  Drummer Max Roach wrote a jazz suite with the song as a musical theme.  BLO members Josh Sperry and Marco Peris created our arrangement, which we play over an Argentinian Murga beat.
